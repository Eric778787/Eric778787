How to make pancakes.

Introduction

I'm trying to teach you how to make a pancake because it will be a good and a fast breakfast if you just follow the steps correctly. I'm going to share you how to make a easy delicious pancake with only 8 steps.

The steps how to make pancakes.

  1. The ingredients are 500g of flour, 2-3 egg, 350ml of milk, 2 spoons of butter, 5 spoons of sugar, half spoon of baking powder and a little bit of salt.
  2. For a tool, you need a pan or a microwave oven.
  3. First you have to check if the ingredients are clean.
  4. You should crack the egg and stir it.
  5. Then you have to melt the butter in a microwave oven.
  6. When the butter is melted, you have to mix the egg and the butter.
  7. If you're done mixing, mix the mixture with the flour, baking powder, milk, sugar, and salt.
  8. You now put the dough on the pan in any size you want. When the pancake is brownish you could move the pancake on a plate and add some topping like honey, nutella, berries etc.

The perfect pancake

  • It should be brownish.
  • It should be a little lumby.
  • It should be soft.

How the pancake shouldn't be like

  • It shouldn't be black.
  • It shouldn't be too thick.
